  • FAQ's

    Are part-worn tyres safe?

    Yes. Each tyre is thoroughly inspected both internally and externally. The tyre will be recycled if any defects are identified.

    Where do you source your tyres?

    The tyres originate from our network including respected retail shops and primary dealers who replace stock leaving many of the tyres in excellent condition.

    Do part-worn tyres invalidate my vehicle insurance?

    No, part-worn tyres may be found on any used car, thus there are no safeguards in place. You can feel confident that our tyres have been thoroughly inspected.

    Other retailers say that part-worn tyres are dangerous. Is this true?

    Not if the tyres have been thoroughly tested. Anyone selling tyres (new or used) should make sure that safety is their top priority.

    Do you offer fitting?

    No however we will attempt to function as a liaison during the process of having your tyre(s) installed, subject to your local area.

    Will my tyre have a puncture repair?

    If a tyre has had a puncture repaired by one of our technicians it will be noted in the description.

    Are puncture repairs safe?

    Depending on where the puncture is and whether it is repaired by a trained technician, a puncture repair is safe.

    What about the age of part-worn tyres?

    Tyres deteriorate with age. We don't sell tyres that are too old, defective, or have aged prematurely. The manufacturer's stamp or more detailed tests can be used to determine the age of a tyre.

    Will my tyres have any imperfections?

    Because of our thorough testing process, we can assure you that you will not be supplied with unsafe tyres. However, due to the nature of part worn tyres, they may have minor blemishes.

    Will my tyres have markings?

    Tyres may have different marks to help our warehouse team with but all stock is detailed to ensure that you receive a tyre that is immaculately presented.

    Why has the term ‘upwards’ been used when describing DOT’s and Tyre Tread depths?

    Because it's difficult to state the precise DOT or tread depth of each piece of stock due to the volume of each piece of stock we carry, we offer the lowest value of the batch's numerical figures, giving our clients a more realistic representation of the product with the intention of under quoting and over delivering.

    How representative are the product images?

    Our in-house team photograph each piece of stock individually to ensure that the product representation is to the most accurate standard.

    Will you send the product to a different address then on the invoice?

    No, the item will only be dispatched to the corresponding address stated on the invoice generated by eBay.

    What delivery method is used?

    Monday through Friday, orders received before 1pm will be dispatched via a 'tracked/signed for' method the same day.

    Will I be given a tracking number?

    Yes, you will be given a tracking number that will allow you to track your delivery.

    Do you offer time slots for delivery?

    We can't guarantee specific delivery slots, but deliveries can occur between 8am. and 7pm. You will be offered the chance to reschedule your delivery via the courier’s website if your first attempt at delivery fails.
